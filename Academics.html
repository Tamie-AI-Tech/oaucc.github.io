Curriculum & Programs

Once Upon A Child Primary School provides students with the full curriculum laid out by the Ministry of Primary and Secondary Education. In addition, the school also offers French lessons to all students from ECD “A” throughout the Primary School grades to grant them the crucial step in being multi-cultural global citizens We have started offering cambridge syllabus up to grade 4 starting 2025. Homework

Students have collaborative homework sessions every afternoon of the school week. During this time the teacher supervises the students assisting each other with their homework online. This helps in the following areas listed below:


  • it builds collaborative culture amongst students
  • leads to the cross pollination of ideas.
  • Fosters adaptability and flexibility etc.
