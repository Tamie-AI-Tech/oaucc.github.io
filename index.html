About Us

Welcome to Once Upon A Child Primary School

Once Upon A Child Primary School (OUAC) was established in 2017 with the sole aim of digitizing children’s education and capacitating them to be global citizens from Primary school

The school started off as a Pre-School and Day Care and has grown since. Currently its most senior class is Grade 4 and each year another grade will be added. The staff and students are excited to be a part of this growing school that is challenging traditional education.

Our Vision

Developing Global citizens and unlocking full potential of each individual child

Our Mission

Capacity enhancement for each child through teaching/learning strategies for both the pupil and the teacher. We ensure a diverse community that fosters mutual respect and social responsibility in partnership with the home for the nation and the global village through an integrated curriculum

What We Offer

We offer cutting edge programs that intergrate technology and morden methods to challenge traditional learning. Our curriculum is designed to develop digital skills and critical thinking, preparing children for a future in an increasingly digital world.

Safety First

We prioritize your child's safety with secure facilities and vigilant supervision

Tech-Enhanced learning Tools

We use computers, online applications and interactive whiteboards to personalise and engage learning

Field Trips

Our field trips offer hands on learning at museums, zoos and exciting, educational destinations.

Healthy Meals and Snacks

We provide nutritiours meals and snacks that support children’s growth, energy and overall well-being

Creative Lessons

Our innovative lessons inspire imagination and critical thinking through hands-on activities and interactive learning experiences

Sports Facilities

Our state-of-the-art sports facility offers diverse activities promoting fitness, teamwork, and healthy lifestyles.

Transportation & Transfers

Transport

The school does not transport students to and from school. However, arrangements have been made with a local private transporter to:

  • Ferry students (to and from City Hall)
  • Pick up and deliver students in the Killarney, Parklands, Kumalo, Suburbs and Selbourne Brooke suburbs

Kindly note that the transport fee is paid separately through the school office and is charged on a monthly basis.

Transfers

One full term’s notice is required from a parent who wishes to transfer their child from OUAC.
