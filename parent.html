School Hours

School opens at 7am. Children can be dropped off from 7am - 07.45 am.

Infants school time

Start time: 08:00am

Dismissal time: 01:00pm

All students should be collected by 2pm. Should parents have challenges, they are advised to inform the school office.

Primary School time

Start time: 07:20am

Dismissal time: 1:00pm [Monday, Wednesday, Friday]

Dismissal time: 3:00pm [Tuesday, Friday to attend sports]

All students are expected to attend their sport activities. Exceptions will be made for those who are medically excused.

Transport

The school does not transport students to and from school. However, arrangements have been made with a local private transporter to: Ferry students (to and from City Hall) Pick up and deliver students in the Killarney, Parklands, Kumalo, Suburbs and Selbourne Brooke suburbs Kindly note that the transport fee is paid separately through the school office and is charged on a monthly basis.

Transfers

One full term’s notice is required from a parent who wishes to transfer their child from OUAC.
